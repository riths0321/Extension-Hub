Privacy Policy for Meet Scheduler

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Meet Scheduler ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension and associated backend services. Our goal is to provide a seamless scheduling experience while respecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide its core functionality, Meet Scheduler interacts with several types of data:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in via Google OAuth, we receive your name, email address, profile picture, and a unique Google ID.
  • Authentication Tokens: We securely store OAuth 2.0 access and refresh tokens to interact with the Google Calendar API on your behalf.
  • Meeting Details: When you schedule a meeting, we process the meeting title, date, time, duration, and the email addresses of any attendees you specify.
  • Usage Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous information about how the extension is used (e.g., frequency of meeting creation) to help us improve the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for the following purposes:

  • Meeting Creation: To create events and generate Google Meet links in your Google Calendar.
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and maintain your session across the extension and our backend.
  • Personalization: To display your profile information within the extension UI.
  • Service Improvement: To monitor system health and optimize the scheduling flow.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your authentication tokens and profile information are stored securely in a Firebase Firestore database. Access and refresh tokens are used solely to communicate with Google APIs. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Services

Meet Scheduler integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs: Used for OAuth 2.0 authentication and Google Calendar event management.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for secure data storage (Firestore) and anonymous usage analytics.
  • Render: Our backend services are hosted on Render's secure cloud infrastructure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information as long as you use the extension. You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account security settings or by contacting us to request data deletion.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@saurabhtiwari.im.
