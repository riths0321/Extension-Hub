# Privacy Policy for Tech Detector Pro

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how **Tech Detector Pro** collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

## Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect personal data.**

**Tech Detector Pro** operates locally on your device to analyze the technologies used by the websites you visit.

### Website Analysis
- The extension scans the code of the current webpage (HTML, scripts, headers) to identify technologies (CMS, frameworks, analytics, etc.).
- This analysis happens entirely within your browser.
- We do **not** transmit report data or browsing history to any external servers.

## Permissions Usage
The extension requests the following permissions for functionality purposes only:

- **activeTab**: Used to access and analyze the content of the currently active tab when you initiate a scan.
- **scripting**: Required to inject the analysis scripts into the webpage to detect technologies.
- **storage**: Used to save your preference settings and recent scan history locally on your device.
- **notifications**: Used to notify you when a scan is complete or if interesting technologies are found (per your settings).

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
