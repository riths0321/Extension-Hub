# Privacy Policy for Accessibility Score Calculator

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how the **Accessibility Score Calculator** Chrome extension collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

## Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect any personal data.**

The **Accessibility Score Calculator** operates entirely locally on your device.
- We do **not** transmit any data to external servers.
- We do **not** collect your browsing history.
- We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

### Reports Storage
The extension stores audit reports locally on your browser using the `storage` permission. This data never leaves your device and is only accessible by you within the extension's dashboard.

## Permissions Usage
The extension requests the following permissions for functionality purposes only:

- **activeTab**: Used to access the content of the current tab when you initiate a scan, allowing the extension to analyze the webpage for accessibility issues.
- **scripting**: Used to inject the analysis scripts into the webpage to perform the accessibility audit.
- **storage**: Used to save your audit reports locally so you can review past scans.
- **<all_urls>**: Required to allow the extension to run its analysis on any webpage you choose to scan.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
